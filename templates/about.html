Sovery: Delivering Deliciousness to Your Doorstep

Sovery is an Indian online food ordering and delivery platform. Besides food delivery, the platform also provides on-demand grocery deliveries under the name Instamart, and a same-day package delivery service called Savory Genie.

This web page uses data to display valuable information about various aspects of customer orders, enabling users to explore and analyze key metrics.

The dataset contains the following attributes:

Column Name Description
Order ID Unique ID for each order
Customer Name Name of the customer
Restaurant ID Unique ID for the restaurant
Order Date Date and time of the order
Quantity of Items Number of items in the order
Order Amount Total amount of the order
Payment Mode Payment method used by the customer
Delivery Time Taken (mins) Time taken to deliver the order
Customer Rating-Food Customer rating for the food
Customer Rating-Delivery Customer rating for the delivery service

The Data page provides a detailed view of customer orders, including their ratings and delivery times.
